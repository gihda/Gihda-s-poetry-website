My First Poem

Where did the road lead me?

Where did the road lead me,
Or should I say, where did I lead myself?
All I wanted was to live a fairytale and smile all the way through.
Others wondered how I stayed so happy,
Others wondered if I ever shed tears.
Well, I am no better than a human with floss,
No better than a mere mortal with fairytale dreams.

But still, I wander with hope in my heart,
Chasing a future, where the road won’t fall apart.
Though the path is uncertain, and the end far away,
I’ll keep walking, day by day.

For the road will lead me where I need to go,

And in my heart, I’ll always know.
The fairytale may be fleeting,
But the joy of the journey is worth repeating.
