Creative Expression

Where did the road lead me

where did the road lead me,
or should i say where did i lead myself?
all i wanted was to live a fairytale and smile all the way through.
others wondered how i stayed so happy,
others wondered if i ever shed tears,
well, i am no better than a human with floss,
no better than a mere mortal with fairytale dreams.

Know You

What is your life's worth?
You live for others?

When are you going to be the master of your very own game?
Instead, you sit at the mercy of people
and seek after their lives as your only source of entertainment.

Oh, darling, you are worth more.
You are so good — you just need guidance
to discover who you truly want to be.

Then, you will find your life's quest.
